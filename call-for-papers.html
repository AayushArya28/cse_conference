IMPACT 2025: Intelligent Machine Processes and AI-Centric Technologies

The First International Conference on Intelligent Machine Processes and AI-Centric Technologies (IMPACT 25) Department of CSE, BIT MESRA, Patna campus

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra (Patna Campus) Bihar, India.

The conference aims to foster knowledge sharing, collaboration, and innovation among researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Emerging Technologies. It will feature keynote speeches, paper presentations, and interactive sessions.

Key Themes:

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

  • Supervised, Unsupervised, and Reinforcement Learning
  • Ensemble Methods and Model Optimization
  • Explainable AI (XAI)
  • AI for Decision Support Systems
  • AI in Edge and Embedded Devices
  • Hybrid Intelligent Systems

Deep Learning and Neural Networks

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), LSTMs, GRUs
  • Transformer Architectures and Attention Mechanisms
  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Lightweight and Efficient Deep Models

Natural Language Processing and Speech Processing

  • Language Modeling and Word Embeddings
  • Text Summarization and Question Answering
  • Sentiment and Emotion Analysis
  • Speech Recognition and Synthesis
  • Multilingual and Low-Resource Language Processing
  • Chatbots and Conversational AI

Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition

  • Object Detection and Recognition
  • Image and Video Processing
  • Facial Recognition and Biometric Systems
  • Scene Understanding and Image Captioning
  • Document Image Analysis and OCR
  • 3D Vision and Augmented Reality

Data Science and Big Data Analytics

  • Data Preprocessing and Feature Engineering
  • Predictive Modeling and Forecasting
  • Statistical Learning and Time Series Analysis
  • Anomaly Detection and Data Mining
  • Scalable Data Pipelines and Streaming Analytics
  • Real-Time Analytics and Visualization

Cloud Computing and Edge Intelligence

  • Cloud-Native AI Systems
  • Federated Learning
  • Edge and Fog Computing Architectures
  • Serverless AI and Model Deployment
  • Resource-Constrained AI Models
  • AI on IoT Edge Devices

Cybersecurity and Blockchain Technologies

  • AI for Threat Detection and Intrusion Prevention
  • Cryptography and Secure Computation
  • Blockchain-Based Identity and Trust Management
  • Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning
  • Secure Federated Learning
  • AI in Cyber Risk Assessment

Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Systems

  • Intelligent Sensor Networks
  • Smart Cities and Smart Homes
  • AI for Smart Healthcare and Wearables
  • Industrial IoT and Automation
  • Context-Aware and Ambient Intelligence
  • IoT Security and Data Integrity

Robotics and Human-Machine Interaction

  • Autonomous Navigation and Control
  • Human-Robot Collaboration
  • AI for Robotics Vision and Sensing
  • Emotion Recognition in HMI
  • Cognitive and Social Robotics
  • Learning from Human Demonstrations

AI for Interdisciplinary and Social Applications

  • AI in Healthcare and Medical Diagnosis
  • AI in Agriculture and Precision Farming
  • AI for Education and e-Learning
  • AI for Environmental Monitoring and Sustainability
  • AI in Finance and Insurance
  • AI for Disaster Management and Crisis Response

AI Tools, Frameworks, and Methodologies

  • Open-Source AI Platforms (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch)
  • MLOps, AutoML, and Model Monitoring
  • Benchmarking and Evaluation Techniques

  • AI Ethics, Fairness, and Bias Mitigation
  • Reproducibility and Responsible AI
