The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra (Patna Campus), invites submissions for the International Conference on Intelligent Machine Processes and AI-Centric Technologies (IMPACT 2025) to be held in Patna, Bihar, India.

It will feature keynote speeches, paper presentations, and interactive sessions.
