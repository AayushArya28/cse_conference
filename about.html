About The Conference

IMPACT 2025 aims to bring together academicians, researchers, industry experts, and students to discuss the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Emerging Technologies. This event will provide a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and fostering industry-academia collaborations. The conference will feature keynote sessions by eminent speakers, paper presentations, and panel discussions. We intend to invite distinguished personalities from premier institutions and industries to enrich the discussions and inspire the participants. This event aligns with the institute's vision of promoting research excellence and innovation.

Key Highlights

  • Keynote addresses by eminent academicians and industry experts.
  • Paper presentations on emerging trends in VLSI, IoT, AI in communication, and more.
  • Panel discussions on the impact of 5G, AI, and future communication technologies.
  • Opportunities for researchers and students to network with industry professionals.
  • Workshops on hands-on applications in embedded systems and signal processing.
  • Exploring innovations in wireless communication, optical networks, and smart computing.
