About The Conference

Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Patna Campus, is organizing a National Conference on "Emerging Trends in Electronics & Communication Engineering (ETECE-2025)". The conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to discuss the latest developments in electronics and communication engineering. The event will feature keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions on cutting-edge technologies in the field.

The rapid advancement in communication and electronic systems has revolutionized various industries. This conference will provide a platform for professionals and scholars to share innovative ideas and discuss technological advancements that are shaping the future of electronics and communication.

Key Highlights

  • Keynote addresses by eminent academicians and industry experts.
  • Paper presentations on emerging trends in VLSI, IoT, AI in communication, and more.
  • Panel discussions on the impact of 5G, AI, and future communication technologies.
  • Opportunities for researchers and students to network with industry professionals.
  • Workshops on hands-on applications in embedded systems and signal processing.
  • Exploring innovations in wireless communication, optical networks, and smart computing.
